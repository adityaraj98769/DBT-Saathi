Confusion clear: Aadhaar Linked ≠ DBT Seeded

Learn the difference, avoid delays, and ensure your scholarship arrives in the right account—fast.

Aadhaar Linked

Bank has your Aadhaar on file. DBT may still fail.

DBT Seeded

Aadhaar mapped to your bank via NPCI. DBT can be routed.

How it works

  1. Link Aadhaar with your bank
  2. Give consent to bank to seed with NPCI
  3. Verify NPCI mapping is Active
  4. Receive DBT in the mapped account
