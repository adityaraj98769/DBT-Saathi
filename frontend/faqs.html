FAQs & Case Studies

Aadhaar linked vs DBT seeded – what's the difference?

• Aadhaar Linked → Your Aadhaar is stored in your bank’s records.
• DBT Seeded → Your Aadhaar is mapped to your bank account in the NPCI database, which is required for Direct Benefit Transfers.b
👉 Example: If you linked Aadhaar at Bank A but seeded Aadhaar at Bank B, your DBT will go to Bank B, not A.

I have linked Aadhaar, but DBT is not received. Why?

•Your NPCI mapping may be inactive or pointing to another bank.
•Always confirm with your chosen bank and submit consent to seed Aadhaar with NPCI.
👉 Check your mapping status on NPCI Aadhaar Mapper or through your bank’s DBT desk.

How do I check if my Aadhaar is DBT seeded?

•Use your bank’s DBT/UIDAI Aadhaar Seeding Checker.
•Visit your bank branch and request a DBT Seeding Confirmation Slip.
•NPCI has a “Check Aadhaar Mapper” service on bank apps/websites.

Can I have Aadhaar seeded in more than one bank?

•❌ Only one bank account can be active at a time in NPCI’s DBT mapper.
•You seed in another bank, the previous one becomes inactive.
👉 Example: If you move from SBI to PNB, your DBT will stop going to SBI once NPCI updates to PNB.

How can I change my DBT receiving bank account?

•A fresh Aadhaar seeding consent form at your new bank.
•Your old bank mapping is updated as inactive.
Confirm with NPCI/Bank SMS that mapping now points to the new account.

What precautions should I take against phishing and fraud?

•Share OTP, PIN, or Aadhaar details on calls/messages.
•Only trust messages from UIDAI, NPCI, or your bank’s official number.
•Always type URLs yourself—don’t click on random links.
•👉 Example: UIDAI SMSes come from UIDAI-VCIP or UIDAI-OTP. Ignore anything else.

What happens if I close my DBT seeded bank account?

•DBT transfers will fail until you seed Aadhaar in another active account.
•Ensure at least one active bank account is NPCI mapped before closing your old account.

Can scholarships fail if Aadhaar is linked but not seeded?

•✅ Linking ≠ Seeding.
•Seeding, the DBT cannot be routed by NPCI.
•Often face delay or rejection of scholarships for this reason.

How much time does Aadhaar seeding take?

•Noramlly 24–48 hours after bank updates NPCI.
•Some cases, up to 7 working days if manual verification is needed.

Where can I get help if DBT is not received?

•Visit your bank branch DBT desk.
•NPCI/Bank helpline.
•The DBT grievance redressal portal of your scheme.
•Keep your Aadhaar number, bank account, and seeding slip ready for quick resolution.
