Aadhaar linked vs DBT seeded â€“ what's the difference?

Linking means the bank stores your Aadhaar. DBT seeded means your Aadhaar is mapped to that bank via NPCI to route DBT payments.

Case: DBT not received although Aadhaar is linked

Likely the NPCI mapping points to another bank or is inactive. Give consent to your chosen bank to seed Aadhaar for DBT and verify mapping.

How can I avoid phishing?

Do not share OTP/PIN. Verify messages only via official bank/UIDAI/NPCI channels. Use the Checker and Learn sections to understand safe steps.
